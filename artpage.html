Artsy with ANSHU!!

"Every chils is an artist.The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up."~Pablo Picaso.

As a kid, school student, I loved sketching, I believed I would live a life of an artist. I would draw in my mind all the time, but my favourite place was on my eating plate. But even before I grew 16, I lost my confidence, I lost the belief on the artistic approach . Today, after 4 years of gap, with absolutely no drawing or sketching, I am once again living my artistic dream fully, daily, tangling on all the tiles. Such is the energy, the flow of tangling, that I end up thanking each pen and paper I come across. Thanks to Zentangle Method.

I wish to make a materpiece someday. A piece of art that would be very close to me and people would know me through that.
